What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency that allows anyone to send money over the internet — no banks or middlemen required. It’s fast, global, and open to all.

Here, you'll find easy-to-follow guides and resources to help you understand how Bitcoin works, why it matters, and how you can get started safely.
